Professional athletes show support for Gordon Hayward after ankle injury

When Gordon Hayward fractured his ankle in the opening minutes of the Celtics' season opener against the Cavaliers, silence fell among the crowd at Quicken Loans Arena. Hayward's Celtics teammates huddled around each other and many players on the Cavaliers appeared shaken as well. According to ESPN's Brian Windhorst, LeBron James checked on Hayward in the locker room as well. Isaiah Thomas, who helped recruit Hayward to Boston, was reportedly there as well.
